# Fitcot Free

**Fitcot Free** is the free version of the **Fitcot** app — a comprehensive fitness and nutrition tracking application built with React Native and Expo.

## What is Fitcot?

**Fitcot** is an all-in-one fitness companion app designed to help users track their workouts, monitor nutrition, set fitness goals, and stay consistent with their health journey. It combines workout logging, meal tracking, detailed statistics, and an AI-powered fitness coach into a single, beautifully designed mobile experience.

### Core Features (Available in Fitcot Free)

- **🏠 Home Dashboard** — A central hub displaying an overview of your daily fitness and nutrition progress at a glance.
- **🏋️ Workout Logging** — Log your workouts with details like exercise type, duration, and intensity.
- **🍎 Nutrition Tracking** — Track your meals and monitor your daily calorie and macronutrient intake.
- **📊 Stats & Analytics** — View detailed statistics and progress charts to visualize your fitness journey over time.
- **⏰ Reminders** — Set custom reminders to stay on track with your workouts and meals.
- **⚙️ Settings & Profile** — Customize your profile, set fitness goals, and personalize your experience.
- **🌙 Dark Mode** — Full light and dark theme support for comfortable use at any time of day.
- **📋 Onboarding** — A guided onboarding flow to help new users get started quickly.

### What's Different from Fitcot (Premium)?

Fitcot Free includes **all core features** of the Fitcot app. The only difference is that the **AI Coach** feature is not available in the free version. The AI Coach in the premium version provides personalized fitness advice, workout suggestions, and nutrition guidance powered by artificial intelligence.
